Frequently asked questions

General

Sivussa is an AI-native visibility team who identify and clear what's preventing your site from being found. You have a website, you pay for hosting, but nobody finds you. Issues suppress your visibility, and paying Google or agencies adds more cost. We audit your site, find what's blocking you, and provide specific recommendations to clear those barriers so you can improve visibility without ad spend. Starting at €89/99.

Pricing

Three plans: One-shot: one full audit with recommendations. Quarterly: audit with recommendations every 90 days. Monthly: audit with recommendations every month.

How It Works

Our AI-native team crawls your site, checks your results in queries, evaluates your content for AI answer readiness, and identifies what's preventing your site from being found. For every issue found, we provide specific recommendations to address it.

Technical

Your website URL, email address, and subscription status. We don't even have access to your personal information from your website visitors, passwords, or database contents.
