## Built in Finland

Sivussa is built by a small team based in Finland. We're bootstrapped, not VC-backed. We believe in minimal infrastructure, AI-powered automation, and direct relationships with customers.

The name "Sivussa" is Finnish — it means "alongside" or "beside." We're alongside businesses that need visibility solutions.

We started Sivussa because we kept seeing the same pattern: businesses running SEO audits, getting overwhelmed by the results, and not knowing how to proceed. The tools weren't helping — they were just generating problems. We built something that provides copy-paste ready fixes.
