SEO tools point out problems. We provide solutions.

## Most tools just audit. We think that's not enough.

Every product on the market does the same thing: audit your site, generate a list of issues, and leave you to figure out the fixes. You built a website, you pay for hosting, but you're not visible. Paying search providers or agencies adds more cost.

You need specific guidance on what's holding you back and how to address it.

## Our approach

We built Sivussa to solve one problem: the gap between finding issues and actually clearing them.

### SEO Agent
Technical health, meta tags, structured data, page speed, content optimization — identifies what's hiding your site from search engines.

### GEO Agent
Google Business Profile, local pack rankings, NAP consistency, local citations — finds what's blocking your local visibility.

### AEO Agent
FAQ content, featured snippets, voice search, AI answer readiness — removes barriers so AI assistants can find and recommend your business.

## Built in Finland

Sivussa is built by a small team based in Finland. We're bootstrapped, not VC-backed. We believe in minimal infrastructure, AI-powered automation, and direct relationships with customers.

The name "Sivussa" is Finnish — it means "alongside" or "beside." We're alongside businesses that want visibility without ad spend.

We started Sivussa because we kept seeing the same pattern: businesses running SEO audits, getting overwhelmed by the results, and not knowing how to proceed. The tools weren't helping — they were just pointing at issues without clearing them. We built something that provides specific, actionable guidance.
