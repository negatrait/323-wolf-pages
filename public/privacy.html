Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

What Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Website URL — The site you want us to audit
  • Email address — To deliver reports and manage your subscription
  • Subscription status — Your plan, payment method, and audit history

How We Use Your Data

  • To crawl and analyze your website's public pages
  • To generate audit reports and recommendations
  • To send scheduled reports based on your subscription
  • To process payments via Stripe

What We Don't Do

  • We don't track your browsing activity
  • We don't sell or share your data with third parties
  • We don't access private or password-protected pages
  • We don't store payment card details (Stripe handles that)

Data Storage

Your reports are stored securely and are private to you. We retain your data for as long as your subscription is active, plus 30 days after cancellation in case you return.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality. We don't use tracking cookies, analytics, or advertising cookies.

Your Rights

You can request:

  • A copy of all data we hold about you
  • Deletion of your data
  • Correction of inaccurate data

Contact us at sivussa@sivussa.com for any data-related requests.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you by email for significant changes.
