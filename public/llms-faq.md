Frequently asked questions about Sivussa SEO, GEO, and AEO audits.

## General

**What is Sivussa?**
Sivussa is an AI-native team of SEO specialists who identify and clear what's preventing your site from being found. You have a website, you pay for hosting, but nobody finds you. Issues suppress your visibility, and paying Google or agencies adds more cost. We audit your site, find what's blocking you, and provide specific recommendations to clear those barriers so you can improve visibility without ad spend. Starting at €89/99.

**How is Sivussa different from Semrush or Ahrefs etc?**
Semrush and Ahrefs are audit tools. They find issues and hand you a checklist. You figure out the fixes yourself. Sivussa is different. Our AI-native team identifies issues and provides specific recommendations to address them. We're not a tool — we're a service that delivers guidance you can implement directly.

**Who is Sivussa for?**
Everyone with a website who wants visibility without ad spend. Small to large companies, SEO professionals, entrepreneurs, bloggers, e-commerce stores, SaaS businesses. If issues are preventing your site from being found, we're here to help clear them.

**Do I need technical skills?**
Basic technical skills help. The audit provides recommendations and guidance. You can implement them yourself or send the report to your developer. Many fixes are straightforward if you're comfortable editing your website.
